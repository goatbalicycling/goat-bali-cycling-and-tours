Bali Downhill Cultural Experience

The island of Bali is a place that begs to be explored on 2 wheels. Embark on a bike tour that takes you from Kintamani to Ubud, making stops along the way at a coffee plantation, traditional family compound and places to eat delicious local fare.

After the pickup from your accommodation, we'll drive to the hilly Kintamani volcano area, where we'll enjoy some of the most amazing natural views. After, we'll stop at a rural school and see how local children spend their days. Strap on your helmet, and begin pedaling away. I will take you to explore parts of Bali rarely seen by tourists, visiting a variety of off-the-beaten-track locations that immerse you in the island's striking beauty. Experience some of Bali's most pristine and untouched landscapes. Visit a local priest compound and interact with the resident.
